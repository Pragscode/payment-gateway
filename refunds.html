Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: December 5, 2025

Refund Policy

Refunds are NOT available for digital subscriptions to our email automation software service. All sales are final for digital products.

When Refunds May Be Offered

In exceptional circumstances, we may consider refund requests if:

  • The service is completely inaccessible due to technical issues on our end for more than 7 consecutive days
  • You contacted support within 24 hours of purchase and the issue cannot be resolved
  • You can demonstrate that our service does not function as described

Grace Period

We offer a 24-hour grace period from the time of purchase. If you request a cancellation within this period and meet the criteria above, we may process a full refund.

How Refund Requests Are Handled

To request a refund:

  1. Contact our support team at support@pragscode.com within the grace period
  2. Provide your order number and reason for the request
  3. Our team will review your request within 5 business days
  4. If approved, refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 7-10 business days

Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account dashboard. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period. No refunds are provided for partial billing periods.

Contact Us

For refund requests or cancellation inquiries, please email us at support@pragscode.com
